# Maintenance Manual for P-101 Centrifugal Pump

## 1. Overview
Asset ID: P-101
Type: Main Centrifugal Pump
Location: Unit 4

## 2. Maintenance Schedule
- **Monthly:** Check vibration levels at the main bearing casing. Normal threshold < 2.5 mm/s.
- **Quarterly:** Inspect mechanical seals for leakage. 
- **Annually:** Complete overhaul and alignment check.

## 3. Troubleshooting
- **High Vibration:** Often caused by bearing wear or cavitation. If vibration exceeds 5.0 mm/s, immediately shut down the pump to prevent catastrophic bearing failure.
- **Seal Leakage:** Can be caused by improper installation or fluid contamination. Replace seals immediately.

## 4. Compliance
All inspections must be logged in Maximo. Any failure to inspect quarterly results in a Level 2 Compliance Violation.
