# Milestone 55 — Portfolio Optimization Engine

## Overview

Milestone 55 transforms the PIA Latent Engine from a counterfactual analysis platform into a prescriptive engineering intelligence platform.

Previous milestones enabled observation, measurement, evidence synthesis, expertise estimation, knowledge modeling, temporal intelligence, forecasting, and counterfactual simulation. However, the platform could only evaluate alternative futures—it could not determine which combination of interventions should be executed.

M55 introduces a deterministic Portfolio Optimization Engine capable of selecting the optimal engineering intervention portfolio based on projected organizational benefit, engineering effort, confidence, and risk.

---

# Objectives

- Introduce Portfolio Optimization as a first-class runtime capability.
- Convert simulated futures into actionable intervention portfolios.
- Rank interventions by expected organizational impact.
- Support deterministic optimization algorithms.
- Generate executive-ready engineering recommendations.
