## Slide 2: The Problem
**Headline:** Heavy Industry runs on isolated systems and tribal knowledge.
**Talking Points:** 
- Shift logs are in binders or unsearchable PDFs.
- SCADA systems throw alarms with no context.
- Maintenance is done blindly on schedules, missing early warnings.
- LLMs hallucinate critical engineering facts, making them unsafe for industrial use.

## Slide 3: The Solution
**Headline:** LLMs understand language. Deterministic engines compute facts.
**Visual:** Architecture Diagram showing Ingestion -> Knowledge Graph -> Deterministic Engines -> Copilot.
**Talking Points:**
- We built a deterministic intelligence platform powered by an Industrial Knowledge Graph.
- The LLM acts *only* as a UI (Copilot), summarizing deterministically computed facts.
- Zero hallucinations. Full provenance to the exact page of the pump manual.

## Slide 4: Key Differentiators
**Headline:** More than just "Chat with your PDFs"
**Bullet Points:**
1. **Universal Ingestion:** Reads P&ID docs, Maximo logs, and sensor alerts.
2. **Deterministic RCA:** Causal engine proves *why* a failure occurred.
3. **Counterfactual Simulation:** "What if we delayed maintenance by 30 days?" - computed mathematically.
4. **100% Provenance:** Every claim is backed by a specific graph traversal.
